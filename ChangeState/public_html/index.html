Family Mission

How to Move:

Use the arrow keys to move the character around. Press space bar to change which character to move.
Some characters can only do certain task.

Note: You can not walk down a ladder (if you get stuck, please use the reset button)

Story:

Alaska(Tall and skinny), Mississipi (Medium and heavy) and Hawaii (tiny) are trying to leave their crazy house, but they need the key in order to unlock the door.
Can you help the family leave? (all family members should exit the open door)
